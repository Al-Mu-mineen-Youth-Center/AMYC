Our Mission

At Al-Mu'mineen Youth Center, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive educational resources and support to refugee youth and new immigrants. We believe every young person deserves the opportunity to thrive in their new home, and we're committed to making that vision a reality.

About Us

Building a brighter future together

Who We Are

Al-Mu'mineen Youth Center (AMYC) is a non-profit organization committed to supporting refugee youth and new immigrants as they navigate their journey in America. We understand the unique challenges faced by young people adapting to a new culture, language, and educational system.

Since our founding, we have served hundreds of families, providing educational resources, mentorship, and community support. Our programs are designed to bridge cultural gaps, enhance academic performance, and foster a sense of belonging.
